About Us

At the Crossroads of Security and Technology

We are proud to present a day of Workshops, Roundtables, and Conversations called CircleCity Crossroads 2020.

This event is geared mainly towards executives. Instead of the normal long list of talks, we are presenting a day of engaging workshops which will invigorate your thirst for learning, roundtables which will provide time for horizon broadening as you discuss shared topics with your peers in a structured session, and the networking opportunities with both vendors and peers will have you walking away with plenty to reflect on and add value to your day to day duties as an executive in IT and Information Security.

We encourage you also to bring a CFO, COO, CEO, CIO, or other executive business partner to share this experience with.

Opening/Keynote: Why Your Security Strategy Isn’t, and How to Fix That

Gabriel Basset
Description

While the concept of security strategy is common, it's actual use is not, either at the organizational or industry level. Let's talk about why we'd want strategy, why we don't have it, and what we can do to get it back.


About Gabriel

Gabriel is a senior information security data scientist on the Verizon Security Research team at Verizon Enterprise Solutions specializing in data science and graph theory applications to cyber security. He is the lead data scientist and a contributing author of the Verizon Data Breach Investigations Report. He supports several information security data science conferences and has previously held cyber security risk management, testing, intelligence, architect, and program management positions at the Missile Defense Agency and Hospital Corporation of America
